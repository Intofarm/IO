Our Story: Cultivating Growth, Harvesting Returns

At INTOFARM©, we bridge the gap between sustainable agriculture and smart investments

Who We Are

Founded in 2012, INTOFARM© began with a simple vision: to make agricultural investments accessible to everyone. Our team combines decades of agribusiness expertise with cutting-edge financial technology to create opportunities that benefit both investors and farming communities worldwide.

We're more than a platform - we're a collective of farmers, economists, and technologists passionate about ethical capital growth and food security.

Our Approach

Direct Project Ownership: Unlike traditional funds, we acquire and operate farmland directly, giving you transparent asset backing.

Tech-Enabled Farming: We deploy IoT sensors, satellite imaging, and data analytics to maximize yields and minimize risk.

Community Impact: 15% of our projects support smallholder farmers through training and fair-trade partnerships.

Why Agriculture?

While markets fluctuate, the world will always need food. Agricultural land has outperformed global stocks by 3.2% annually since 2000 (FAO data), with lower volatility. Our model captures this stability while addressing critical challenges:

  • Global food demand to increase 50% by 2050
  • Only 12% of arable land produces optimally
  • Institutional investors own less than 1% of farmland - we're changing that
